Fire Kings

A corner dedicated to the Fire King Yugioh cards

How to play

The Fire King Deck is built around destroying your own fire monsters to gain an advantage. Some cards like "Fire King High Avatar Kirin" and "Fire King Avatar Arvata" have effects that can only activate when they are destroyed, and cards like "Legendary Fire King Ponix" and "Sacred Fire King Garunix" can be summoned for free when a fire monster gets destroyed.
